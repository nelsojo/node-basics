The most interesting fact about you or your most unique experience

In July of 2020 I took part in a robotics competition hosted by NCAS (Nasa Community College Aerospace Scholars). I learned much about space explopration and took part in a competition where teams built small scale robotic rovers and programmed them to collect objects from a mock mars terrain course.
